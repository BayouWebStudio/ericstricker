About Eric Stricker

Valencia, Spain

Where Mathematics Meets Mysticism

Eric Stricker doesn't just tattoo — he engineers impossible realities onto skin. From his private studio in the sun-drenched Mediterranean city of Valencia, he has spent years refining an unmistakable visual language: sacred geometry rendered with hypnotic precision, dotwork so meticulous it borders on obsessive, and fractal blackwork patterns that seem to fold inward on themselves like Escher illustrations come to life.

His work draws from the deep mathematics of nature — Fibonacci spirals, tessellated grids, Platonic solids — and translates them into large-scale bodysuits and full sleeves that transform the human form into a living geometric monument. Faces dissolve into crystalline structures. Organic curves merge with impossible angles. Each piece is a meditation on the threshold between order and chaos.

International clients travel from across Europe, the Americas, and Asia for his multi-session projects, often committing months or years to realize a single vision. Eric works exclusively by appointment, approaching each collaboration as an architectural endeavor — meticulously planned, precisely executed, and designed to age with quiet permanence.

His studio is sparse and deliberate, much like his work. No flash on the walls. No walk-ins. Just focused silence and the hum of the machine, building impossible worlds one dot at a time.
