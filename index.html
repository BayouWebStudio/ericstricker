The Artist

Geometry
Has a Pulse

Eric Stricker transforms the human body into a canvas of impossible dimensions. Working from his private studio in Valencia, Spain, he specializes in sacred geometry, fractal blackwork, and dotwork — creating Escher-like tessellations that breathe and shift with the body's form.

International clients fly in from across the globe for his full bodysuits and sleeves, each piece a months-long collaboration between artist and wearer.
